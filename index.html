About TEDxPUP
What TEDx is and this year's theme
October 18, 2025 • Manila

Reimagining Tomorrow

TEDxPUP is an independently organized TED event that brings local voices and global ideas together. This year's theme explores how communities reimagine systems, education, and technology to build brighter, more inclusive futures.

Expect thought-provoking talks, collaborative workshops, and networking breaks designed to spark action.
